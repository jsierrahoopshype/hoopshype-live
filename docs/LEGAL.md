# HoopsHype Live — Legal Checklist

## What We Display and Why It's Safe

| Content | Source | Status | Notes |
|---------|--------|--------|-------|
| Live scores & box scores | nba_api (NBA.com stats) | ✅ | Facts are not copyrightable; non-commercial use |
| Headlines (text only) | HoopsHype Rumors | ✅ | Internal team permission; headlines only, no article text |
| Rankings tables | Your Google Sheets | ✅ | Your own data |
| Bluesky posts | Public Bluesky API | ✅ | Public posts with full attribution (name, handle) |
| NBA team names | Common usage | ✅ | Factual references, not trademark use |
| Fonts | Google Fonts (Outfit, DM Sans, DM Mono) | ✅ | Open Font License |

## What We Do NOT Display

- Full article text from any source
- Copyrighted images or NBA logos (we use text abbreviations)
- Paywalled content
- Private or protected social media posts
- Any content requiring authentication to access

## Attribution

- Bluesky posts are displayed with author name, handle, and timestamp
- Headlines are attributed to HoopsHype
- All data sources are identified in the UI
